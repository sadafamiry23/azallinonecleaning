Last-Minute & Add-On Services

Need something extra? We're ready to help — even today.

Book Now Contact Now

What Does "Last-Minute" Mean?

Last-minute cleaning means we’re ready to act fast. If you call us today needing your home cleaned by 4PM — we can make it happen. Whether it’s unexpected guests, a surprise party, or a moving emergency, our team is on standby. We deploy reliable professionals who arrive quickly and deliver spotless results, even with tight timelines.

What Are Add-On Services?

Our add-ons allow you to customize your cleaning exactly how you want it. Whether it’s deep-cleaning appliances, sprucing up overlooked spaces, or tackling fur-covered furniture — we’ve got the flexibility to enhance your base service. Add as many extras as you need, whenever you need them.

Kitchen Add-Ons: Beyond Oven & Fridge

Our kitchen cleaning goes far beyond wiping appliances. We sanitize counters, degrease backsplash tiles, wipe cabinets inside and out, scrub the sink and faucet, polish stainless steel, clean light switches, empty and clean trash bins, and even dust vents and baseboards. We also clean microwave interiors, disinfect handles, mop floors, and shine light fixtures. It’s a full reset for your kitchen — leaving no spot untouched.

Pet Hair & Upholstery Refresh

Pets are family — but their fur gets everywhere. Our pet hair removal targets sofas, rugs, chairs, stairs, and even hard-to-reach corners. Using advanced tools, we extract embedded fur and dander to reduce allergens and restore freshness. Perfect for homes with shedding breeds, allergy concerns, or post-pet visits.

Window & Glass Cleaning

Our streak-free glass cleaning restores clarity and brightness to any room. We clean both interior and exterior glass surfaces, including windows, patio doors, mirrors, and storefront panels. We wipe sills, edges, and tracks. Ideal for sunrooms or businesses needing polished presentation.

Garage & Entry Area Sweep-Out

Garages and entryways collect clutter fast. Our sweep-out service removes dirt, leaves, cobwebs, and debris. We clean floors, pressure wash where needed, dust shelving, wipe light switches, and freshen the air. These overlooked areas will match the cleanliness of your indoor spaces.

Laundry Services

Don’t stress over laundry. We offer assistance with washing, drying, folding, and organizing your clothes. Whether you have baskets full of clothes or need help after moving, our team takes care of linens, towels, and everyday wear. Everything is returned neatly folded and ready to put away — handled with care and discretion.

Need It Today? Let Us Know.

We accommodate urgent requests whenever possible. Book online or contact us to confirm availability!
