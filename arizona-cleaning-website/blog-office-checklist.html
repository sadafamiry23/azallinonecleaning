Office Cleaning Checklist for Every Season

Stay organized year-round with this seasonal office cleaning guide

Keeping your workplace clean is essential for productivity, employee health, and professional appearance. Here‚Äôs a simple checklist organized by season to help you stay ahead.

Spring:

  • Deep clean carpets and upholstery
  • Disinfect all desks, phones, and shared surfaces
  • Wash windows inside and out

Summer:

  • Dust air vents and clean ceiling fans
  • Wipe down office kitchen and break areas
  • Check and restock hand sanitizers and supplies

Fall:

  • Clean baseboards, corners, and under furniture
  • Sanitize keyboards and electronics
  • Replace HVAC filters and clean light fixtures

Winter:

  • Increase frequency of floor mopping due to weather
  • Disinfect door handles and high-touch points daily
  • Clean windows and doors to remove salt/residue
