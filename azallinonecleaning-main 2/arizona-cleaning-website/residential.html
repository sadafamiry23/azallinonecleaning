Residential Cleaning

What is Residential Cleaning?

Our Residential Cleaning Services are designed to deliver a clean, healthy, and stress-free living environment. Whether you need a one-time deep clean or a recurring schedule, we tailor each session to your lifestyle. Our team ensures every kitchen, bathroom, and living space gets the care it deserves.

Residential / One-Time Cleaning

  • Detailed dusting and surface wipe-down
  • Vacuum and mop floors in all rooms
  • Disinfect high-touch areas
  • Trash removal and deodorizing

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Wipe and disinfect counters, appliances, and sinks
  • Clean cabinet faces and backsplash
  • Degrease stove tops and clean microwave interior
  • Take out trash and mop floors

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Scrub sinks, tubs, showers, and toilets
  • Sanitize fixtures and countertops
  • Shine mirrors and chrome
  • Empty trash and deodorize

Interior & Common Area Cleaning

  • Dust furniture, décor, and fixtures
  • Vacuum rugs and carpet
  • Clean windows (interior), doors, and mirrors
  • Organize and straighten shared spaces

Whole House Touch-Up

  • Quick clean of key surfaces and areas
  • Ideal between scheduled visits or before guests arrive
  • Fluff pillows, straighten furniture, and wipe smudges

Deep Cleaning

  • Top-to-bottom refresh of the entire home
  • Baseboards, vents, fans, blinds, and hard-to-reach spots
  • Inside of cabinets, drawers, and appliances
  • Recommended for first-time or seasonal clients

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

  • Empty home cleaning for moving day transitions
  • Wall scrubbing, floor polishing, and disinfecting
  • Inside cabinets, drawers, closets, and appliances
  • Ensures readiness for lease or new tenants
